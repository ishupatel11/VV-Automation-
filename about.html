Our Vision

V.V. Automation was established in the year 1987. V.V. Automation gradually expanded its business of Sale and Purchase of Used Machines for industrial & engineering use to the whole country. We provide a transparent and satisfactory experience to our customers. This extends from a single piece of equipment to a complete plant. Customer satisfaction is always our number one priority.

V.V. Automation are well armored with technological competence to establish long term relationships with our clients. For the past 30 years, we are serving heavy industries and are maintaining harmonious relationships with our customers.
